What Cultural Relativism is and is not
======================================
**** TUESDAY - QUIZ ON UTILITARIANISM OVER VIDEO AND PP 233-238 ****
What are the claims of Cultural Relativism?
- Culture A (Greeks)
  - Burn their dead
- Culture B (Collatians)
  - Eat their dead
- Culture C (Chinese)
  - 3 year mourning
- The Relativist says that none of these are either right nor wrong
  - There is no _objective_ way to settle the disagreement
  - It's a matter of opinion
