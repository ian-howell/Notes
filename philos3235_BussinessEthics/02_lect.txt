Moral Dilemmas - Trolley Problem
================================

What is Moral Theory?
---------------------
- Deals with weighting out moral values
  - primary: self-interest, consequences, strict principles, virtues

- Moral Dilemmas - show a conflict of values - have to choose one or the other
  - which do I think is more important and why?

Trolley Car Problem
-------------------
- There is a trolley car moving along a track
  - The track forks off int 2 tracks: A and B
- There are 5 people standing on track A
- There is 1 person standing on track B

- Should the trolley driver switch from track A to track B?
  - Utilitarian approach - "Ought implies Can"
